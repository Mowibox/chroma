Hello everyone! As promised when launching the site, **Chroma is now officially open for contributions!**

Whether you want to share ideas, suggest improvements, or add your own tutorials, any help is welcome.

## How to contribute?

* **:speech_balloon: Share your feedback on Discord:** use the [Review and Feedback](https://discord.com/channels/1383790297594728518/1386648737366937701) channel to ask questions, suggest improvements, or discuss ideas with contributors.
  
* **:warning: Report issues on GitHub:** found a bug? Create an [issue on Chroma's GitHub](https://github.com/Mowibox/chroma/issues/new/choose) so it can be tracked and fixed.
  
* **:pencil: Write tutorials or help with website development:** Contribute new tutorials, improve existing ones, or dive into the code to enhance the site.

## Complete guide

A detailed guide on how to contribute is available here: [How to contribute to Chroma?](https://mowibox.github.io/chroma/en/tutorials/miscellaneous/how_to_contribute/)
